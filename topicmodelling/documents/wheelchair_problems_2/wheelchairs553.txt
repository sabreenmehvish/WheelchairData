Sports wear and products What products and sports wear need to be designed for those that are disabled? Do standard Underarmor, Nike, Etc. products serve as adequate products? What products do you wish were made for you when you play sports? It's all about the functional waist. When making pants, shorts, tights, whatever, the front of the waist needs to be lower than the back. Take a look at the way ABL cuts the waist on their jeans.  As a tennis and rugby athlete, I go through gloves like a bear coming out of hibernation. There is a great need for gloves that can withstand the constant friction from the push rims and don't cost an arm and a leg. My longest lasting pair was borrowed from the football aisle here in the US
